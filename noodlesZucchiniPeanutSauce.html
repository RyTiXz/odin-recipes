Ingredients

  • 2 onions
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 dried chili pepper
  • 1 large zucchini
  • 300 ml vegetable broth
  • 80 g peanut cream
  • 1 lime
  • salt, pepper, sugar
  • 0.5 bunch fresh parsley
  • 500 g spirelli

Steps

  1. Peel garlic cloves and chop finely like the chili pepper (wash hands!). Peel the onions, halve and cut into fine rings. Wash the zucchini thoroughly and then divide into thin sticks.
  2. Squeeze the lime and set liquid aside. Clean and coarsely chop the parsley. Sauté the onions, garlic, chili pepper and zucchini briefly in a pan with a little oil and set aside or remove from the pan and keep warm.
  3. Meanwhile, boil the noodles according to package directions.
  4. Heat the peanut cream, lime juice and vegetable stock in the pan until the peanut mixture has completely dissolved. Season to taste with a little salt, pepper and sugar. Then add the vegetables to the sauce and increase the temperature again briefly.
